FAQ

Does it support huge PDFs?

Yes, our system is designed to handle very large PDF documents with ease. By default, the limit is set to 300MB which covers most professional and academic use cases. However, if you are working with even larger files such as scanned books, heavy research papers, or image-rich catalogs, you can increase this limit by adjusting the server configuration. This flexibility ensures that you are never restricted, no matter how big or complex your documents are.

Will layout be pixel‑perfect?

For the majority of documents, the conversion engine maintains excellent text fidelity and ensures the structure of your file is preserved. That means headings, paragraphs, and tables remain clean and readable after conversion. But in the case of highly complex PDFs that contain intricate designs, charts, or non-standard fonts, achieving a 100% pixel-perfect replica may be difficult. If your project demands absolute perfection in layout and formatting — identical to the original file — we recommend integrating a LibreOffice-based microservice as an additional layer, which can guarantee exact formatting preservation down to the smallest details.
